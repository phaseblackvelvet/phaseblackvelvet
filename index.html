Artist Bio

Sound as texture

Phase Black Velvet is a production studio and solo artist project rooted in the intersection of Progressive / Melodic House / Techno, and cinematic electronic music. Every release is built from the floor up—tight kicks, submerged bass, and hypnotic motifs that unfold slowly across the night.

From warehouse sessions to headphone journeys, the work prioritizes space, tension, and release. The studio handles composition, mixing, mastering prep, and sound design for artists who want a nocturnal, forward-leaning aesthetic without losing warmth.
